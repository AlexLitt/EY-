Discover and activate the DNA of EY’s highest performing teams

Creating the highest performing teams is central to our Vision 2020 ambition. We must attract, empower and inspire the best people and sustain a culture of world-class, highest performing teams that help create a lasting impact on our work and our clients’ work.

Our highest performing teams have three common characteristics:

    Shared vision — the teams articulate a bold, clear vision that engages and inspires everyone

    Right mix — the teams select, respect and develop a diverse mix of talent with the right skills at the right time

    Quality results — the teams set high standards expected of our profession and enable each individual and team to deliver quality results
